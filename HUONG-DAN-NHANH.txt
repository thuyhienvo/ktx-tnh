  (Ban chay KHONG can Docker)
========================================

MO APP:
  - Nhap doi (double-click) file:  Chay-App.bat
  - Doi vai giay, trinh duyet tu mo http://localhost:3000
  - GIU cua so den (console) mo trong khi dung app.

DANG NHAP LAN DAU:
  - Tai khoan: admin
  - Mat khau: admin123
  - (Vao roi nen doi mat khau ngay o muc Cai dat)

TAT APP:
  - Dong cua so den, HOAC nhap doi file:  Dung-App.bat

TRANG DANG KY CHO HOC VIEN MOI (cong khai, khong can dang nhap):
  - http://localhost:3000/dang-ky
  - Hoc vien dien thong tin -> gui don. Quan ly vao muc "Don tu HV"
    de duyet va bam "Them vao phong" (app se tao tai khoan dang nhap).
  - May khac trong mang: http://<IP-may-nay>:3000/dang-ky

CAI LEN MAY NHU 1 APP (tuy chon):
  - Mo http://localhost:3000 bang Chrome/Edge
  - Bam bieu tuong "Cai dat" tren thanh dia chi -> Cai dat

DU LIEU LUU O DAU:
  - Toan bo du lieu nam trong thu muc:  .data
  - SAO LUU = copy ca thu muc .data sang noi khac.
  - PHUC HOI = chep .data tro lai.

CAC MAY KHAC TRONG CONG TY MUON DUNG:
  - Mo: http://<dia-chi-IP-may-nay>:3000
  - (Xem IP bang lenh ipconfig)

LUU Y:
  - Ban nay chay bang Node ban goi san trong thu muc .runtime,
    KHONG can cai dat gi them, KHONG can quyen admin.
  - Khi nao cong ty bat duoc Docker/WSL thi co the chuyen sang
    ban Docker + PostgreSQL (xem README.md) de manh hon.
