ABOUT US

At Maison Panthere we are inspired by a mission to create exceptional places that enrich every guest’s sense of home away from home with a more personalized and authentic experience.

Much more, we celebrate the individual traveller’s heart, embrace simplicity, highlight the locally made, environmentally friendly, organic and sustainable and share our deep passion for the unique and genuine. And most of it all, we seek to continuously spark love for the Beauty of the Cote d’azur, all season long.
